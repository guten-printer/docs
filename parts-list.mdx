## Required

- [ESP32-WROOM-32 Development Board](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07WCG1PLV)
- [USB-A to Micro USB Cable](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0711PVX6Z)  
- [ESP32 Breakout Board](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0BNQ85GF3)
- [Maikrt Embedded Thermal Printer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B77LB8VZ)
- [Adjustable Power Supply](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08BL4QMGM) (any 12~24V/3A power supply works)
- [BPA/BPS-Free Thermal Paper Rolls](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CMQBZQH5) (any 57x30mm thermal paper rolls work)

## Optional
- 3D Printer (for printing the case)
